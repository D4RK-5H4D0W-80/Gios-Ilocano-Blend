ABOUT

JEZZI'S FOOD PRODUCT is the manufacturer of the "Nagasang Ken Naraman" products or the Gio's Ilocano Blend spices. It is an Ilocano's finest chili garlic sauce in Santiago , Ilocos Sur.


OUR STORY

Our company started in La Union with only one product and our first market is our co-employees and friends way back 2012. It was then, we continued producing our products as part-time for our extra income. But, one day I meet this business man who believes in our product and he make us used his business permit to expand our markets. How this business man ended in believing our product? Simply not just because of the good taste but it is a consumable products, meaning “masarap na, sigurado bibili at bibili ulit ang taong makakatikim ng produkto na eto” That quote of this business man stock up in our mind to push more. At first, some carinderia and some small stores in La Union are added in our list of market. But with our aim to get bigger market, in 2014 we have the biggest market which is the CSI city mall in San Fernando City La Union and Monterey Meat Shop Plus all over La Union. Since our product is a homemade, we planned to expand and make it fulltime, so we decided to resigned from work and went home to Ilocos Sur. 2017 we decided to have our own business permit because some of our clients now are looking for our permits and specially on FDA permit.

Innovating our marketing strategy, we have come up with 3 new products and introduce it to the market. It was very successful because how our first product were patronized it’s the same on the new ones.

Pandemic comes, we thought our operation will be affected but we felt the increase of demands because of the online sellers. In the mid of pandemic we have built our small production area because it is one of the requirements in getting an FDA license. We have to ask assistance to DOST because they have a program for food processing. We pass the assessment on this agency and granted us to upgrade our equipment for faster production and as a requirement for food safety standards. 2021 of July we already have our LICENSE TO OPERATE issued by FDA. With the help of DTI and DOST we are now introduce to different markets here in Ilocos Sur and joining also the trade fair of DTI for additional product exposure until we were introduce by an exporter last December 2021.
